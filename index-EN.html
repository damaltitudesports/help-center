Order Processing

Once you’ve placed your order on Altitude Sports, the processing time will differ depending on when you place it. Note that the team processes orders between Monday and Friday, 9am to 5pm EST. If you place an order Friday night processing will begin Monday morning. The processing time is already incorporated into the aforementioned delivery time when you select your preferred method of shipping.

Estimated Delivery Date

After choosing your preferred shipping method and courier, please check for your estimated delivery time in the first step of your shopping cart. Estimated delivery information is provided by the courier company. The processing time is already incorporated into the delivery time, but weekends and statutory holidays are not counted. Within Canada, we guarantee that your delivery will be made by the time indicated. Note that your delivery may arrive before the estimated delivery time provided by the courier company.

If your order is shipped within Canada, and it arrives after the estimated delivery indicated on Altitude Sports, as provided by the courier company at the time the order was placed, we will gladly reimburse you the shipping fees you may have paid. Delays to orders due to circumstances out of our control, such as snowstorms, hurricanes, credit card verification procedures or postal strikes, will not be counted against the allocated time for delivery.

For deliveries to the United States, and other countries around the world, the estimated delivery time cannot be guaranteed by Altitude Sports. Due to the fact courier companies sub-contract shipments to local companies, and the unknown variable of customs processing times, delivery times can be unpredictable and in rare instances delayed. For more information concerning this subject, please read the following section.

In-Store Pickup: a practical and free option

If you happen to live near Mont-Tremblant, you may also pick up your order from that store, without paying any shipping fees. Simply choose this option when you are at the stage of choosing your shipping method in your shopping cart. Expect a delay of 7 to 8 business days for this shipping option. This delay exists because transfers from our warehouse to the stores occur only once a week. You will be contacted by telephone once the store has received your order. For security reasons, we oblige you to bring a printout of your receipt, the credit card used to pay for the order, and 2 pieces of identification when you pick up your order. You will also be asked to sign a document attesting to the pickup.

Receiving and picking up orders

For all receptions of orders shipped with FedEx, we require a signature confirming that you have indeed received your order, and not a stranger. This protects both you and Altitude Sports from cases of theft or conflict in the case of a delivery error.

Although estimated delivery dates are almost always respected, we suggest you leave a window of 3 business days between the date of delivery and the date you absolutely require your order. This way if the courier company arrives when you are out, you will have ample time to pick it up from the post office. Furthermore, if a natural disaster, postal strike or any other event we cannot control should occur, the 3 business day window will not leave you high and dry. Please use forethought to prevent any unnecessary stress, because we do not want you leaving on your trip to the Alps without that new backpack.

The courier companies we use to ship your orders deliver throughout the day, but usually between 9am and 6pm. We suggest having your order delivered to your usual daytime location. 90-day return policy

We do our best to guarantee you’re completely satisfied with all orders placed on Altitude Sports. To ensure this, we allow product returns for a refund within 90 days of receiving your order.

Product must be in perfect condition

All product must be returned to Altitude Sports in perfect condition. This means the product will:

  • Have all of the original labels and tags
  • Be in the original packaging and have the original hanger (if applicable)
  • Be as clean as when initially shipped and have no unusual odors or fur/hair on it
  • Be tried-on over underwear for hygienic reasons (particularly products like swimsuits and undergarments)

Please be aware that all products are thoroughly inspected upon return.

Altitude Sports reserves the right to refuse returns that do not respect these conditions and ship the product back to the customer at their expense. In some cases at our discretion, we may impose restocking fees.

We do reimbursements; not exchanges

Our experience dictates it's more advantageous for everyone that we do not offer exchanges because it's simpler to reimburse you for returned product. It's quicker and easier to consider each transaction separately; this way you don't need to wait until your refund is complete to order the correct product. If you’d prefer another size or color, simply order that style and send back the unwanted product at your earliest convenience. Placing this second order eliminates the need to wait for your refund to process, and ensures you get the correct product delivered to your door as soon as possible.

Example: If you wish to return a jacket in size small because you think the medium will fit better, you should do two things. First, return the small jacket. You’ll be refunded when that process is complete. Second, order the medium jacket, which will be delivered via the usual shipping process.

Advice: Order multiple sizes, colors, or styles of the same product.

If you're hesitating between sizes, colors, or models, we advise ordering several products so you can compare them, then just return the products you don’t like. This single order will limit your number of transactions and save time, all while ensuring you receive the specific product you want. It also guarantees you'll get the item you want before it sells out.

Altitude Sports stocks many different brands and models, but certain products may be limited in quantities or colors. During our sales, specific styles sell out quickly.

Price Policy

Altitude Sports is compelled to offer our customers the best pricing. If you have an inquiry about price matching or are requesting a pricing adjustment on a previously placed order, please contact our customer service team. One of our team members will gladly assist you.

All price matching requests must meet the following conditions:

  • If you find the product you purchased from Altitude Sports is priced lower by our competitor, or if that product's price has decreased on our website within 15 days of your purchase, we will refund your account the price difference in Alti Dollars.
  • We only consider a price matching competitor to be a North American-based business that is also an authorized dealer of the brand and product in question. A competitor must also be authorized to ship that brand and product to Canada.
  • The product must be identical in brand, model, sizing, color, and from the same year of fabrication.
  • The product must be in stock and available for immediate purchase from our competitor at the price you are requesting. We will not match out of stock products or sale pricing that has expired.
  • Our customer service agents must be able to verify the requested price match via telephone or internet from the competitor. Verbal agreements are not eligible.
  • When matching our competitor's product price, we include shipping fees charged by a competitor that apply to the cost of the product we have been requested to price match. Meaning, if a competitor is selling a product for 200$ plus a 50$ shipping fee, we will consider the total price matching cost to be 250$. This rule applies to any additional charges that we incur due to our price matching policy.
  • We only offer price matching in Canadian Dollars. If a price match is requested on a product listed in American currency, we will use the daily US-to-Canadian dollar exchange rate based on the Bank of Canada to calculate the total cost of a product.
  • Auction sites, liquidation and clearance sales, used products, demo or floor models, and unofficial websites are not eligible for price matching. We also do not match product prices from The Last Hunt or Amazon.
  • Altitude Sports matches pricing on our website and in our store. We also match product pricing from both online and traditional retailers that meet our price matching requirements listed above.
  • You can return a product we’ve price-matched for you. If you wish to purchase the same product (same colour and same year of fabrication) in a different size, but the item is no longer on sale, matching the lower price on a replacement item is entirely at our discretion. Be aware return shipping fees may apply.
  • You can return a product we’ve price matched for you. If you wish to purchase the same product (same color and year of fabrication) in a different size, but the item is no longer on sale, we will gladly uphold the adjusted lower price of that product. Be aware return shipping fees may apply.
  • We reserve the right to limit the number of products purchased under our price matching policy.
  • Our price matching cannot be combined with any additional discounts or promotions. Only the 5% membership discount (if applicable) can be applied to the matched price of a product.
  • Our Lowest Price Guarantee does not apply to products sold by Marketplace sellers (see Osprey seller page).

Please don't hesitate to contact one of our customer service representatives if you have further questions.
